# Green IA Platform

## Overview
Green IA is a multi-tenant SaaS platform for CBD commerce. It combines e-commerce, point-of-sale, AI-powered shopping assistance, and analytics into a single operational system for regulated wellness brands.

## Features
- Multi-tenant storefront management
- Product catalog and category management
- Real-time stock syncing between online and POS
- AI Budtender assistant powered by Gemini
- Semantic and vector search for product discovery
- Loyalty, subscriptions, referrals, and review modules
- SEO-ready architecture with JSON-LD structured data

## Technology
- React 19 + Vite + TypeScript frontend
- TailwindCSS design system
- Supabase backend (auth, database, storage)
- Gemini AI integration for conversational commerce
- Vector embeddings for semantic retrieval

## Use cases
- Launching a compliant CBD e-commerce store quickly
- Running omnichannel CBD operations with POS + online sync
- Improving conversion through AI recommendations
- Scaling content and organic traffic with programmatic SEO
- Supporting customer questions via conversational AI and voice search

## Target users
- CBD store owners and franchise operators
- E-commerce managers in regulated wellness categories
- Growth marketers focused on organic acquisition
- Product and operations teams standardizing retail workflows
